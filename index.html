614acmw.github.io

614ACMW is a new local ACM-W professional chapter, focused on the advocacy, support and celebration of Professional Women in Computing. Our meetings will be a mix of in-person and online, and will include tech talks, professional networking, career support, socializing, and fun technical activities. Our chapter will buddy with the NW Ohio ACM-W, OCWiC ACMW, and ACM-W Student Chapters in central Ohio.

What are the member requirements?

Support our mission to support, advocate and celebrate women in computing. No dues, minimum participation or committee responsibilities are required. We welcome women, men, non-binary, academic, and industry technology professionals at all levels. <\p>

What are the member benefits?

  • Early notice of upcoming events
  • Member only events
  • ACM-W newsletter
  • ACM tech and career newsletter
  • Input/vote on events, officers, activities, meets etc.
  • Bragging rights for being a founding member
  • Small swag, like stickers, posters, notes and pens
  • Member Badge image for email and webpage
  • Member Badge pin
